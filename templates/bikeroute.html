THE BIKE ROUTE
Explore the diversity of Amsterdam by bike
The Bike Route was curated especially for you by a born and raised Amsterdammer and a team of international students studying in Amsterdam. There are 12 pinpoint locations scattered across the city, with various sights to see and stops to make along the way. Bike at any pace, stop at any place, so what are you waiting for, start your adventure!
BEGIN ROUTE
Vondelpark
Situated in the middle of the city is Vondelpark, opened in 1865 and a national monument since 1996. Vondelpark offers a variety of attractions, such as cafés, playgrounds, fountains, and many more. Whether you want to cycle, picnic or relax, this green oasis is the perfect place to be.
Museumplein
Museumplein is the Dutch name for “Museum Square”, the cultural heart of Amsterdam. Here you can admire the works at the Rijksmuseum, Stedelijk Museum, Van Gogh Museum and Moco Museum, or enjoy a concert at the Concertgebouw. A must-see for any and all lovers of art.
Albert Heijn
Stock up on snacks, drinks and local specialties at "Appies", the largest supermarket chain in the Netherlands. Don’t miss the staples of the Dutch culture; cheese, stroopwafels, and frikandelbroodjes. With many stores in the city, you'll always be able to find one nearby.
Brouwerij Troost
Brouwerij Troost is a brewpub famous for their locally brewed and locally loved beers. Try their organic drinks, meat-free food and even pour your own pilsner from their wall tap. Experience and enjoy Amsterdam’s craft beer culture from a local favourite.
Albert Cuyp Markt
Take a stroll down Albert Cuypstraat, the largest daily market in Europe with over 260 stalls. Established in 1905, the market has something for everyone, and is suited for people of all ages. The market is situated in the heart of De Pijp, a vibrant and multicultural neighborhood in Amsterdam.
Joe & The Juice
Peckish for a bite? Joe's got you covered. Come for the fresh juices, healthy shakes and delicious sandwiches. Perhaps if you're lucky, you'll be served by the best latte artist in town. People have traveled far and wide for the signature Mika grin. It's the best part of JoJo's, guaranteed.
The Upside Down
The largest interactive social media museum in Amsterdam, where you make the rules. Imagine a museum, an amusement park, a nightclub, a few perfect backdrops and some magic mushrooms blended together to create the ultimate Dutch experience.
The TFIOS Bench
At the corner of Leidsegracht and Herengracht, two of the most picturesque canals in the city, you'll find the TFIOS bench. This bench was once featured in a movie, where the main characters had a heartfelt conversation. It is now become a symbol of love and hope for many visitors.
Beatrixpark
As one of the nicest parks in Amsterdam, the design of Beatrixpark is a monument of romanticism meets modern functionalism. See if you can spot its orginial romantic character of small lakes and open landscapes, muchly influenced after the English.
