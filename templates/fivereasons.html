DISCOVER THE CITY BY BIKE
Experiencing the iconic cycling culture of Amsterdam
Amsterdam is one of the best cities to explore on two wheels due to its deeply ingrained biking culture. Biking allows for a more authentic and engaging experience, as it lets you explore at your own pace, and immerse yourself in the bustling city. Although Amsterdam has a reliable public transport system, biking is a unique and fun way to discover the city's beauty and history. So if you're looking for an energetic and cultural adventure, why not choose to bike your way through Amsterdam?
5 REASONS WHY YOU SHOULD BIKE
Bike-friendly city
Amsterdam is a city where biking is truly loved by everyone! With its well-developed cycling infrastructure that includes an extensive network of bike paths, bike lanes, and traffic-calmed streets, it is one of the most bike-friendly cities in the world. Just remember to always watch out for cars, trams, pedestrians, and other cyclists to ensure a safe and fun biking experience!
Health and Sustainability
Explore Amsterdam in an eco-friendly and healthy way by chooisng biking as your mode of transport! Not only can you reduce your carbon footprint, but you can also stay active and energized during your travel.
Cultural Experience
Biking in Amsterdam is a cultural experience in itself. You can immerse yourself in the local way of life and learn about the city's rich cycling history and traditions!
Sightseeing Opportunities
Want to travel Amsterdam's charming neighborhoods, stunning canals, and historic landmarks at a leisurely pace? Biking is a perfect way, as you'll have plenty of opportunities for sightseeing and photography along the way.
Convenience
With bike rental shops scattered throughout the city, you can easily rent a bike to explore Amsterdam in the most convenient and cost-effective way possible. So find the nearest bike rental shop, rent your bike, and start pedaling!
